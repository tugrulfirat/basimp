Tools

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

▬

Solid Redaction

Draw a box over any text, name, email, or number. Solid fill — no brush strokes, no partial covers. Proper redaction.

◈

Blur Region

Select any area and apply a strong Gaussian blur. Perfect for faces, backgrounds, or anything you want obscured but not blacked out.

⊞

Pixelate

The classic privacy tool. Select an area and pixelate it — looks intentional, not accidental.

✂

Crop

Draw to crop. No dialog boxes, no entering pixel values — just drag the area you want to keep.

↗

Arrows & Annotations

Point at things. Add arrows in any color and size to highlight issues, explain UI, or direct attention.

T

Text Overlay

Click anywhere to add text. Choose size and color. Great for labeling, captioning, or adding watermarks.

✏

Free Draw

Brush tool for freehand marks — circle something, cross it out, highlight a region with color.

📋

Paste & Copy Clipboard

Ctrl+V to paste a screenshot directly. One click to copy the result back. Zero file management.

Workflow

Three steps. Ten seconds.

1

Paste or Open

Ctrl+V to paste from clipboard, or open any PNG / JPG / WebP file.

2

Edit

Pick a tool — redact, blur, crop, annotate. Draw on the canvas. Undo if needed.

3

Export

Copy to clipboard or download as PNG/JPG. Back to Slack, email, or wherever you need it.
